Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This app collects basic user data to improve user experience.

We do not sell or share your personal data.

Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal data, including your daily check-ins, sobriety tracking metrics, and journal entries, for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide you with the app's services.

If you choose to delete your account, all associated user data and user-generated content will be permanently removed from our active databases and servers within 30 days, as outlined in our Data Deletion section.

Account & Data Deletion

Users can request the deletion of their account and all associated data at any time. To request deletion, please email us at anchorforhoperecovery@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request."

Upon receiving your request, we will delete your account information and any user-generated content from our servers within 30 days.
